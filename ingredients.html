Monk Fruit & Stevia

Sweet without the crash. Naturally.

We keep it clean, and that includes how we sweeten. No cane sugar, no artificial stuff. Just Monk Fruit and Stevia, two naturally derived, zero-calorie sweeteners that do the job without messing with your blood sugar or gut.

Stevia is extracted from the leaves of the stevia plant and it doesn't spike your blood sugar, and it's been used in traditional cultures for centuries.

Monk Fruit (also known as Luo Han Guo) is a small green melon used in Eastern medicine. It gets its sweetness from antioxidants called mogrosides, not sugar, so it's gut-friendly and safe for all ages. Together, they bring just the right touch of sweetness to your protein without the bitter aftertaste or the blood sugar rollercoaster.

Transparency Note on Natural Flavours

We use natural flavours in our protein powders and omega-3-algal to enhance taste — always in minimal amounts (less than 0.75%). In some cases, these flavours may contain a small amount of a carrier like maltodextrin to maintain consistency and stability. We've worked hard to keep it to the bare minimum and continue to explore cleaner alternatives. As always, our commitment is to full transparency and keeping every ingredient purposeful.
